AI VS ML

Within the last decade, the terms
artificial intelligence (AI) and machine
learning (ML) have become buzzwords that
are often used interchangeably.
While AI and ML are inextricably linked
and share similar characteristics,
they are not the same thing. Rather, ML is a major subset of AI
